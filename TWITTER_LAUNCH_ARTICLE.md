## ⚠️ IMPORTANT: Use Telegram Web (Desktop Browser)

**For the best experience, please use Telegram Web on your desktop browser, NOT the mobile Telegram app.**

### Why Telegram Web?

- ✅ **Smooth Wallet Integration** — Browser extensions (like MetaMask) work seamlessly  
- ✅ **No Deep Link Issues** — Avoids common mobile wallet connection problems  
- ✅ **Better Performance** — Faster loading and smoother interactions  
- ✅ **More Reliable** — Test all features end-to-end without interruptions

### How to Access Telegram Web:

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
2. Go to **[web.telegram.org](https://web.telegram.org)**
3. Log in with your phone number
4. Search for **`@compoundminiapp`**
5. Click **Open** to launch the app

**Note:** While the mobile Telegram app works, you may experience wallet connection issues. We strongly recommend using Telegram Web for the smoothest experience.

## 🚀 How to Get Started

### Step 1: Open the App

1. Go to **[web.telegram.org](https://web.telegram.org)** in your browser
2. Search for **`@compoundminiapp`** in Telegram
3. Click **Open** to launch the Mini App

### Step 2: Choose Your Mode

**Option A: Tour as Guest** (Recommended for first-time users)
- Explore the entire app without connecting a wallet
- View all features, rates, and interface
- Perfect for understanding how it works before committing funds

**Option B: Connect Wallet**
- Full functionality with real transactions
- Requires a wallet with Sepolia testnet ETH (for testing)
- MetaMask browser extension recommended for Telegram Web

### Step 3: Start Using DeFi

Once connected:
- **Supply** WETH to start earning interest
- **Borrow** USDC against your collateral
- **Monitor** your health factor and portfolio
- **Withdraw** or **Repay** anytime

## 🔐 Wallet Setup

### Recommended: MetaMask Browser Extension

1. Install MetaMask from [metamask.io](https://metamask.io)
2. Add the extension to your browser
3. Switch to **Sepolia Testnet** (for testing)
4. Get Sepolia ETH from a faucet if needed
5. Connect in the Compound Mini App

The app supports multiple wallets via Reown AppKit (WalletConnect), so you can use MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Rainbow, Coinbase Wallet, and more.

## 📊 What You Can Do

### Supply Assets
Deposit WETH into Compound Protocol and start earning interest automatically. Your supplied assets are tracked in real-time, and you can see your APY update live.

### Borrow Against Collateral
Need liquidity? Borrow USDC against your supplied WETH. The app calculates your borrowing power and health factor automatically, so you always know your position's risk level.

### Monitor Your Portfolio
The dashboard shows everything at a glance:
- Total supplied assets
- Total borrowed amount
- Current health factor
- Real-time APY rates
- Available borrowing power

### Manage Risk
Health factor monitoring ensures you never over-leverage. The app warns you when your position approaches risky thresholds, giving you time to adjust.
